Complete NIST Standard Coverage

Aegis implements all algorithms finalized by the US National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST), ensuring your applications meet the highest standards for quantum resistance and long-term compliance.

ML-KEM (Kyber) ‚ú®

Key Encapsulation Mechanism

The primary standard for establishing secure communication channels, replacing traditional methods like RSA and Diffie-Hellman key exchange.

ML-DSA (Dilithium) ‚ú®

Digital Signature Algorithm

The primary standard for verifying the authenticity and integrity of digital messages and software, serving as the main replacement for RSA or ECDSA signatures.

SLH-DSA (SPHINCS+) ‚ú®

Digital Signature Algorithm

A stateless hash-based signature scheme providing a conservative, well-understood security foundation as an alternative to lattice-based methods.

Also includes full support for secondary and experimental standards like FN-DSA, HQC-KEM, and Classic McEliece for research purposes.
